Frances Burton

Frances Burton is a management and communication skills trainer and consultant with 30 years experience, who has:

  • experience of working with a wide range of clients, to identify and meet their learning and development needs
  • expertise in designing and delivering effective training programmes for groups and individuals
  • qualified as a coach and works with individuals and groups.

Key Experience

In the course of her career, she has:

  • designed and delivered a wide variety of management and skills based courses.
  • facilitated managers’ learning and development through coaching.
  • facilitated action learning sets as part of the National Action Learning for Managers programme.
  • managed a Citizens Advice Bureau in the City of London.

Career Review

1987 to date - management and communication skills trainer and consultant

The areas in which she has designed and delivered training for organisations include:

  • developing effective managers and leaders
  • identifying and meeting client needs
  • business and strategic planning
  • managing change
  • leading high performing teams
  • performance management – supervision and appraisal
  • dealing with difficult clients
  • managing in a multi-cultural environment

The areas in which she has designed and delivered training for individuals include:

  • communication
  • negotiation
  • public speaking
  • team working
  • coaching and mentoring
  • delegation
  • time and stress management
  • change management

Her consultancy work has included: business planning and development, team building, organisational reviews and coaching individuals and groups.

1985-1987 – Manager of City Citizens Advice Bureau in London

Recruited, trained, supported and supervised the bureau staff.

1982-1985 – Co-ordinator for the Society of Voluntary Associates

Recruited, trained and deployed volunteers to mentor young offenders and young people ‘at risk’.

1981-1982 – Financial Management Trainee with South-West Thames Regional Health Authority

1979-1981 – Assistant Probation Officer with the Inner London Probation and After-Care service

Other unpaid roles

  • school governor
  • member of a trustee board for a Citizens Advice Bureau
  • director of Talk About Productions, a community based theatre company

Professional Training and Qualifications

1992: Foundation and Part One in accountancy with the Chartered Institute of Public Finance and Accountancy
